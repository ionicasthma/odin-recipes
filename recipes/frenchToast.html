frenchToast

Description

This is the best frenchToast in the world! Its a must try!

Ingredients

  • 8 thick slices bread
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup milk
    •

Steps

  1. 1. Preheat griddle to 350 degrees F or to heat a skillet over medium heat
  2. 2. Add all ingredient,except the bread, to a blender or to a shallow dish and whisk well to combine. If whisking by hand, mix flour and eggs together first, then add remaining ingredients.
  3. 3. Dip bread slices into the batter, dredging them well on both sides, and place on hot, greased griddle or skillet.
  4. 4. Cook for a few minutes, until the bottom of the breads starts to get golden brown. Flip and cook on the other side the same.
  5. 5. Remove to a plate. Serve warm with syrup and a sprinkle of powdered sugar
